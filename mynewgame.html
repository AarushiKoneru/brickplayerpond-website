Brick BREAKER Pong-Multiplayer!

By: Aarushi

Brick BREAKER Pong is a game coded by Aarushi using CODE.org.

The game is excatly like the og Pong BUT there is a twist!

The there are a wall of bricks which make the game harder and more difficult to control the TWO balls.

In this game you have two players, P1 and P2(p1 is the bottom screen).

Instructions: a and d to move P2, right and left arrow keys to move p1.

First to 3 points wins.

I hope you enjoy my game!
